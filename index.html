Sameer Patil

I am a technologist turned astrophysicist based in Navi Mumbai, India.

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Astrophysics at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay under the guidance of Prof. Varun Bhalerao, where I focus on high-energy transients, particularly gamma-ray bursts (GRBs). Additionally, I collaborate with Prof. Girish Kulkarni at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) on extracting intergalactic medium (IGM) properties from the 21-cm forest signal.

My work heavily involves applying AI/ML techniques to solve problems in astronomy, with particular interest in deep neural networks, CNNs, U-Nets, denoising, inference, and XGBoost.
